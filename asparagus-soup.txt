Asparagus Soup
==============

Ingredients
-----------

* 1 potato
* 2 leeks
* 400g asparagus
* 600ml vegetable stock
* 100ml greek yogurt
* 50lml double cream
* 1tsp sugar
* Salt and pepper
* Butter

Instructions
------------

Slice the potato, leeks and asparagus (except the tips).

Saute in butter for 5 minutes.

Add vegetable stock. Season with salt, pepper and sugar.

Simmer for 40 minutes.

Add asparagus tips and simmer for 10 more minutes.

Blend or mash.

Stir in the yogurt and cream and let it cool.

Serve chilled.
